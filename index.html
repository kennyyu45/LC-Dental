At LC Dental, we provide comprehensive dental care including Periodontics, Implants, and Orthodontics. As a referral-based practice, our patients come to us through recommendations from satisfied patients and trusted medical professionals.

We never double-schedule appointments, so you'll always be seen on time and receive our full, undivided attention. Our eco-friendly facility features digital radiography that reduces x-ray exposure by 85%, a next-generation ultrasonic scaler, and 3D animation software to help you make confident, informed decisions about your care.

Oral cancer screenings are performed for every patient as part of their routine exam.
